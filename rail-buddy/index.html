Incident History
The History tab stores a timeline of past incidents and disruptions across the MRT and LRT network, complete with timestamps and details.
Perfect for checking if that morning delay has been resolved or reviewing the timeline of a major disruption.
Step 3 of 4
First & Last Trains
Use the Timings tab to look up first and last train times for any station on any MRT or LRT line.
Great for planning early morning commutes or making sure you catch the last train home.
