Track Your Spending
Tap Add (the + button) to log a transaction. Choose Deductions for expenses and Additions for income or top-ups. Every entry is tagged to the current month automatically.
Your Allocated amount is the budget ceiling. Used tracks total spending, and Remaining shows what's left.
Step 3 of 3
New Month Reset
At the start of a new month, tap New Month Reset on the main screen. This restarts your balance from zero â€” but all your transaction history is kept and still browsable via the month tabs.
The reset doesn't delete anything. It simply sets a new baseline so your current balance reflects only this month's activity.
