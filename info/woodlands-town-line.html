The Woodlands Town Line formerly (Woodlands City Line, WDL City Line & Woodlands Central Line) is a fictional line based in Woodlands Town, Singapore (not real life). This MRT Line serves Balmoral & Woodneuk. The project is based on the OMSI 2 version of Woodlands Town.
Woodlands Town Line is formerly owned by SMB3087R Studios & SG5574R and acquired by Transit Singapore & SMRTO Ltd, rebranded WCRT to WCRT Operations to avoid association with Yishun LRT's WCRT.
