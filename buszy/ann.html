Service Adjustments - DTL

Early Closures & Late Openings (ECLO) along the entire Downtown Line (DTL), between Bukit Panjang and Expo, will take place across nine consecutive Fridays & Saturdays between 10 July 2026 and 4 September 2026.
Service Adjustments - TEL
28 Apr 2026

Early Closures & Late Openings (ECLO) along the entire Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL), between Woodlands North and Bayshore, will take place across seven consecutive Fridays & Saturdays between 22 May 2026 and 4 July 2026.

20 Feb 2026

The West Inner Loop of the Sengkang LRT Line will be closed from 19 April 2026 to 18 October 2026. This is to facilitate the track works needed to integrate the existing LRT line with the expanded Sengkang LRT Depot.
