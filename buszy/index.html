Step 1 of 5
Pin a Bus Stop
Use the search bar to find any bus stop by name or code, then tap the pin icon on a result to save it here for quick access.
Switch to the Nearby tab to discover stops around your current location without typing anything.
Step 2 of 5
Check Live Timings
Tap any pinned bus stop to view real-time arrival timings, including the next few buses and their seat load status.
Timings auto-refresh every 2 seconds by default. You can adjust this interval in the Settings page.
Step 3 of 5
Updates & More
Tap Updates in the menu to see bus service alerts and announcements. Switch to the Alerts tab to view disruptions and route changes. Use Timings to look up first and last bus times for any stop.
The updates icon in the menu will pulse when there's a new alert or announcement.
Step 4 of 5
Customise Settings
Head to Menu > Settings to change the time format, toggle display options like fleet legend and incoming buses, and control notification behaviour.
You can adjust what you want to see and the way Bus Service information is displayed, and export your pinned stops as a backup from the Settings page.
Step 5 of 5
Bus Arrival Notifications
On any bus arrival timings page, tap the Notify button to get notified when that bus is approaching your stop.
Control how long Buszy watches for your bus under Menu > Settings > Notify Mode — choose once, 24 hours, or always-on.
