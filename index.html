SMRT Virtual

  About Us

We are a Simulated Virtual Transport Operator

SMRT Virtual is an OMSI 2/OpenBVE simulation, based off SMRT's Trains, Light-Rail and Buses, as well as Land Transport Authority's innovations. We are in no way affiliated with SMRT, just inspired by them.

Replicating as close as the real-life

Here you'll get to see our designs and concepts, being implemented in OMSI 2 and OpenBVE. You will also get to see our ideas and products being showcased. We are not affiliated with the real-life SMRT. Currently on an indefinite hiatus, due to personal commitments.

All you need to know about SMRT Virtual

SMRT Virtual operates over 6 OMSI 2 Maps and 5 OpenBVE Train Routes

Buses - OMSI 2



Hillgrove Town

Hillgrove Town is a map made in-house by SMV Studios. This map includes 15 drivable services – 301, 302, 307, 308, 971, 973, 974, 975, 976, 979, 983, 984, 989, 990 and 991.

Woodlands Town

Woodlands Town is a map made in-house by RRTS Studios, but taken over from where they have left off, by SMV Studios. This map includes 15 drivable services – 401, 402, 858, 881, 882, 883, 885, 886, 889, 901, 902, 903, 911/T and 965.

Berlin (BRT)

Berlin BRT is an add-on DLC map. This map includes 17 drivable services – X1, X10, 110, 115, 285, 601, 620, 621, 623, 625, 626, M19, M29, N10, N12, N13, N84.

Lemmental

Lemmental is a freeware map. This map includes 20 drivable services – 100, SB51, SB52, 450, 451, 452, 453, 454, 455, 456, 457, 458, 459, NE40, N41, N42, N43, N44, N45, N46.

Great Grundorf 2

Great Grundorf 2 is a map made by 3DTransportStudio, with upgrades made in-house by SMV Studios. This map includes 34 drivable services – 66, 67/E, 68, 69, 70/E, 71, 72, 73, 74, 75, 76, 77, 78/E, 79, 91, 92, 93, 94, P1, P2, P3, N67, N68, N73, A1, A2, A3, A4, E1, E2, E3 and E3.

Hohenkirchen

Hohenkirchen is an add-on DLC map. This map includes 4 drivable services – 849, 852, 854 and 855.

Airport Express Line is a map made by Nickgoh45, with upgrades done in-house by SMV Studios.

Yishun LRT

Yishun LRT is a map made by willis_chong, with upgrades done in-house by SMV Studios.
